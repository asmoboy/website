Shipping policy

This placeholder policy summarises how orders are dispatched and delivered. It is demonstration content.

1Dispatch times

In-stock orders are dispatched within 24–48 hours of payment clearing. Orders placed before the daily cut-off enter same-day picking.

2Rates & free shipping

Standard tracked shipping is a flat rate and free on orders over €150. Express cold-chain and international options are available at checkout.

3Packaging

All orders ship discreetly with no product references outside. Compounds requiring cold chain are insulated and gel-packed.

4Tracking

A tracking link is emailed when your parcel is handed to the carrier. Transit times vary by destination and carrier.

5Returns

Because these are sensitive research materials, we cannot accept returns of opened vials. Contact us within 48 hours if an order arrives damaged or incorrect and we’ll make it right.
